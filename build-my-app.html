About Your Custom App

Pro subscribers and above can have their own versions of the Noovvo Apple and Android apps. The Android apps have their own listing on Google Play but due to Apple’s rules the iOS app will be listed as an unbranded container app which then becomes custom branded to your company and logo as soon as logged into for the first time.
